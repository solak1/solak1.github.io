Premium Databases

Canisius College subscribes to top quality services that professionals in the field use. The Canisius Library allows students to use AICPA, FASB, and IRS resourses.

Thomas Reuter's Checkpoint

Checkpoint provides users the ability to research auditing and accounting topics. It is made for tax and accounting professionals with a slant towards tax and advisory services.

Breadth

Federal Tax, State & Local Tax, Estate Planning, Pension & Benefits, International Tax, Payroll, Accounting, and Audit & Corporate Finance

Depth

Checkpoint is a great resource for tax professionals. Any student looking to solve a tax issue visit Checkpoint first.

Accessibility

Checkpoint can be accessed via the articles & data section on the library website. The layout is modern and clean. The search function is advanced, but easy to learn thanks to the intuitive design. Searches rank relevance on a three star basis.

FASB ASC Professional View

Financial Accounting Standards Board (FASB) and its recent Accounting Standards Coditification (ASC) are available on the Profession View.

Breadth

The FASB ASC is the bible of CPAs. The ASC deliberately describes the recording of all financial items. The ASC should be used to avoid accounting errors.

Depth

FASB ASC Professional View includes broad definitions while also addressing specific accounting problems.

Accessibility

The ASC Professional View can be accessed via the FASB's website. The layout is not optimized for the modern web. The side bar allows users to quickly access indexed sections of the Codification. Users can serach by code or by word/phrase. The search function works and returns relevant searches with a clickable title. The titles are followed by short descriptions.

IRS Audit Technique Guides

Internal Revenue Service (IRS) updates and maintains Audit Technique Guides (ATG) in order to help IRS agents run audits. The guides can be used in order to see exactly what the IRS would do during an office or field audit.

Breadth

The IRS ATG only covers specific issues that are common in industries. IRS agents will follow the technique guides directly. Therefore, these are helpful to comply with the tax code.

Depth

The IRS ATGs are specific to each industry. They discuss industry specific issues, business practices, and terminology.

Accessibility

The Audit Technique Guides are accessed via the IRS's website. The layout is user friendly. Users can quickly jump to letters, but there is no specific search function to the ATGs.
